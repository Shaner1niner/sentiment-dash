How to use SETA

Use SETA to separate attention from validation. Attention shows where participation is active. Decision pressure shows where sentiment behavior and price behavior are meaningfully aligned or out of balance.

What “structure” means

On this site, structure means price/sentiment structure: whether price, sentiment, attention, volume, and confirmation are forming a coherent setup or still disagreeing.

Production Refresh

The site is hosted on GitHub Pages. Refresh runs update static dashboard payloads and the context packet after safety and smoke gates pass.

Not a trading signal

SETA is a research and education layer. It is not a price forecast, buy/sell recommendation, or financial advice.
