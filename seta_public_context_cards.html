SETA Market Context

Sentiment beneath price, translated into plain language.

These cards summarize how SETA reads attention, sentiment, and price/sentiment structure by asset. Decision Pressure is the model’s read of how sentiment behavior and price behavior are aligning, diverging, or waiting for confirmation. These are educational explanations, not predictions, recommendations, or trade signals.

Attention is not validation.High attention means participation is active. It does not mean price has confirmed the behavior.
Sentiment vs price matters.Decision pressure compares sentiment behavior with price behavior to show where they are aligned, stretched, or out of balance.
Structure means confirmation.Price/sentiment structure asks whether attention, sentiment, price, volume, and regime are forming a coherent context or still disagreeing.
